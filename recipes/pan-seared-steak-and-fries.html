Steak Frites Recipe

Description

This Pan Seared Steak and Fries recipe is a classic and satisfying meal, featuring a perfectly seared steak paired with crispy homemade fries. Ideal for a hearty dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • Fresh rosemary or thyme sprigs
  • 4 large potatoes, cut into fries
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Season steaks generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over high heat. Add steaks and sear for 3-4 minutes on each side.
  3. Add butter, garlic, and herbs to the skillet. Baste steaks with the melted butter for an additional 2 minutes.
  4. Remove steaks from skillet and let rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  6. Fry potato fries in batches until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels and season with salt.
  7. Serve steaks with fries on the side.
