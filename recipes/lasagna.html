Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a family favorite, featuring layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey cheese. Perfect for a comforting dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Layer 3 noodles, half of the ricotta, half of the mozzarella, and a third of the meat sauce. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining noodles, meat sauce, and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes. Let cool for 15 minutes before serving.
