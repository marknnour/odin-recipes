Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Description

This Spaghetti Bolognese is a rich and hearty Italian classic, featuring a savory meat sauce served over al dente spaghetti. Perfect for a family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, red wine, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions; drain.
  4. Serve sauce over spaghetti and top with grated Parmesan cheese.
